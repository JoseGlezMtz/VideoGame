Our idea

Forest Adventure is a Trading Card Game made by three students inspired by the popular Disney series Gravity Falls where you get the chance to select your team and defend the campsite! Forest Adventure" is a TCG that tells the story of our seven characters trying to defend their camp which is suddenly invaded by creatures from another dimension.... themselves. Our campers and staff have the responsibility to defend the camp at all costs with the help of multiple power ups and each other. Join Chef, Wendy, Brick, Lumberjack, Coach, Mike and Lifeguard on their epic adventure!

To begin playing simply:

  1. Log in to your existing account if this isn't your first time playing or start by creating an account
  2. Build your deck if you wish to select which characters to use or jump sraight into action if you're eager to play
  3. Once the game starts, use your team's abilities to boost your team or attack the enemies. Remember to use your power ups to make your team stronger and good luck!
